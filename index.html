The Logbook

Las Vegas · 2024

Neon till 4 a.m.

Nevada · the Strip · zero sleep

Rolled into Vegas with a full tank and no plan. Spent the night chasing reflections of the Strip in the tank, then watched the desert swallow the lights on the way out. The city you come for; the silence after is the part you keep.

Red Rock · 2024

Red Rock loop at sunrise

Nevada · 21 km of curves · 30 min from the Strip

Twenty minutes from the slot machines and suddenly it's just red sandstone and empty switchbacks. Best warm-up ride I've found anywhere — cold air, warm light, no traffic.

Valley of Fire · 2023

Mars, but an hour away

Nevada · 90 min out · otherworldly
